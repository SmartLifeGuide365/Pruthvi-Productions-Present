Reel 06 — Your Questions, Answered

Before You Reach Out

How do we book Pruthvi Productions for our wedding?+

Message us on WhatsApp with your wedding date and location — we'll walk you through packages, confirm availability, and lock your date with an advance booking amount.

Do you travel for destination weddings outside Goa and Konkan?+

Yes. While we're rooted in Goa, Sawantwadi, Sindhudurg and the Konkan coast, we travel across India — and abroad, on request — for destination weddings. Travel and accommodation are added to your package.

How long until we receive our photos and film?+

You'll get a curated sneak peek within the first week, with your full edited gallery and cinematic film delivered on a timeline we confirm at booking — typically within 4–6 weeks depending on the package.

Can we customize a package?+

Every package is a starting point. Add extra hours, a second shooter, drone coverage or an album, or drop what you don't need — we'll tailor it on a quick WhatsApp chat.

Which locations do you cover?+

We're rooted in Goa, Sawantwadi, Sindhudurg and the wider Konkan coast, and regularly shoot weddings across Maharashtra and beyond for destination bookings.
